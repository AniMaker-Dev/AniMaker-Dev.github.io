AniMaker: Multi-Agent Animated Storytelling with MCTS-Driven Clip Generation

A framework for generating coherent, long-form animation from text with multi-candidate video generation, intelligent selection, and global story-level consistency.

Haoyuan Shi Yunxin Li Xinyu Chen Longyue Wang Baotian Hu* Min Zhang

* Corresponding Authors
